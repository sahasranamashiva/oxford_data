    'Adverse' or 'Averse'?

    Which of the following is correct?

    • I\'m not averse to discussing the matter
    • I\'m not adverse to discussing the matter

    Which of the following is correct?

    • You can leave if the weather\'s averse
    • You can leave if the weather\'s adverse

    Which of the following is correct?

    • There was no adverse impact on service
    • There was no averse impact on service

    Which of the following is correct?

    • Many are very computer averse
    • Many are very computer adverse

    Which of the following is correct?

    • It\'s the long hours that I\'m averse to
    • It\'s the long hours that I\'m adverse to

    Which of the following is correct?

    • She had an adverse reaction to nuts
    • She had an averse reaction to nuts

    Which of the following is correct?

    • She rejected the averse findings
    • She rejected the adverse findings

    Which of the following is correct?

    • Check your car in averse weather
    • Check your car in adverse weather

    Which of the following is correct?

    • An arrest may lead to adverse publicity
    • An arrest may lead to averse publicity

    Which of the following is correct?

    • Are you averse to exercise?
    • Are you adverse to exercise?
